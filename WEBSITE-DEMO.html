Our story

Built around the counter, not the cash register.

We opened with one idea: a cafe should feel like the best part of your neighborhood, not a stop on your commute. Every cup is pulled to order, every pastry is baked before sunrise, and every regular gets remembered — name, order, and all.

We keep the menu focused on purpose: four coffees done exceptionally well, five teas for every mood, a snack case worth lingering over, and drinks for everyone who walks in without caffeine on their mind.

  • Locally roasted beans, weekly
  • Baked fresh, every morning
  • Free wifi, no time limit
  • Dog-friendly patio seating
